Tournament Mode

Tournament Mode is when there are enough humans (as opposed to robots) playing that the game “counts”. Usually this is 4 or more humans per side.

Once you are in tournament mode, you will earn “Damage Inflicted” with every ship killed, every army bombed, and every planet captured. Sufficient damage inflicted, plus a high enough offense rating (damage inflicted per hour) will result in “promotions” to higher rank. If you are not playing as a guest, these promotions will persist to future games on the same server.
