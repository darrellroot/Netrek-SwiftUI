Strategy and tactics

The goal of the game is to genocide the enemy team by capturing all 10 of their planets (while preventing them from capturing yours). Here are ways to help this:

  • Bomb their excess armies (more than 4 on a planet). This way they use them cannot capture your planets.

  • Prevent them from bombing your armies.

  • Use your excess armies to capture their planets.

  • Control your space, preventing them from capturing your planets.

  • Kill their players with kills. Only players with kills can carry armies and capture planets.

  • Work as a team to destroy ships. 2 ships usually kill 1 ship in a dogfight. This includes coordinating with your team by reading and writing on your team message board (bottom right).
